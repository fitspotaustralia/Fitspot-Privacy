Privacy Policy

Welcome to Fitspot! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our fitness class booking application.

By using Fitspot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you create an account with Fitspot using Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Name: Your display name from your Google account
  • Email Address: Your Google account email address
  • Profile Photo: Your Google account profile picture (optional)
  • Phone Number: If you choose to provide it (optional)
  • User ID: A unique identifier created by our authentication system

2. Booking and Activity Information

We collect information about your use of the app:

  • Class Bookings: Classes you book, attend, or cancel
  • Video Views: Workout videos you watch and your viewing history
  • Login Activity: Dates and times of your app usage
  • App Interactions: Features you use and content you access

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect body measurements or health data
  • We do NOT collect precise GPS location data
  • We do NOT collect payment information (handled by third-party processors if applicable)
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers or marketing companies

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your Fitspot account
  • Class Booking: To process and manage your fitness class reservations
  • Communication: To send you booking confirmations, class reminders, and important app updates
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • App Improvement: To understand app usage and improve features and user experience

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Use your data for purposes other than app functionality
  • Track you across other websites or apps

How We Store and Protect Your Data

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase and Cloud Firestore, industry-leading cloud platforms that provide:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Encryption at Rest: All data stored on our servers is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use Google Cloud's secure, certified data centers
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access user data when necessary for support

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Third-Party Services and SDKs

Fitspot uses the following third-party services and Software Development Kits (SDKs) to provide and improve our app functionality:

1. Google Firebase Services

We use several Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user authentication via Google Sign-In
    Data collected: Email address, user ID, authentication tokens
  • Cloud Firestore: For secure data storage
    Data collected: Only the data you provide (as described in this policy)
  • Firebase Analytics: For understanding app usage and improving user experience
    Data collected: App interactions, device information (model, OS version), general location (country/city level), crash data, session duration
    Purpose: App performance monitoring, feature usage analysis, crash detection
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For detecting and fixing app crashes
    Data collected: Crash logs, device state at time of crash, device identifiers, app version
    Purpose: Identifying and fixing bugs to improve app stability
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): For sending push notifications
    Data collected: Device tokens, notification interaction data
    Purpose: Delivering class reminders, new content alerts, and important updates
  • Firebase Storage: For storing and serving media files
    Data collected: Uploaded files (if any), access metadata
    Purpose: Storing profile photos and app content

Firebase Privacy: Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Firebase Analytics data is used solely for improving our app and is not shared with third parties for advertising. Learn more: Firebase Privacy and Security

2. Google Sign-In

  • Purpose: Secure OAuth authentication
  • Data collected: Name, email, profile photo (with your permission)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

3. YouTube Player SDK

  • Purpose: Embedding and playing workout video content
  • Data collected: Video viewing behavior, device information (collected by YouTube)
  • Note: When you watch videos in Fitspot, YouTube may collect data according to their privacy policy
  • Privacy Policy: YouTube Privacy Policy

4. Data Collected by Third-Party SDKs

The third-party SDKs integrated into Fitspot may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, device identifiers
  • App Usage Data: Features used, session duration, screen views
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, loading times
  • Network Information: IP address (for general location, not precise), network type

Important Notes About Third-Party Data Collection:

  • Firebase Analytics data is used only for app improvement, not for advertising
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Crash and analytics data is anonymized when possible
  • You can opt out of analytics tracking in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements; Android: Settings → Google → Ads)

Third-Party Responsibility: We do not control how these third-party services process data. Each service operates according to its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies through the links provided above.

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

You can view and update your personal information at any time through your Fitspot account settings.

Control Your Notifications

You can manage notification preferences in the app settings to control:

  • New content notifications
  • Class reminders
  • Promotional messages

Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. You can do this by:

  • In-App: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Email: Contact us at fitspotaustralia@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account"
  • Web Form: Submit a deletion request through our Account Deletion Form

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will permanently delete your data within 30 days.

Export Your Data

You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at fitspotaustralia@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Fitspot is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Sending you an in-app notification or email (for significant changes)

Your continued use of Fitspot after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Users

Fitspot is operated from Australia and caters primarily to Australian users. If you are accessing our app from outside Australia, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Australia or other countries where our service providers operate. By using Fitspot, you consent to such transfer and processing.
